FAQ

What are the most frequently asked questions of TORO Trucking Academy?

Do you offer financing?
We do not offer direct financing through the school, however we work with several funding agencies including the VA who offer tuition assistance to those who qualify. To see if you qualify, stop into one of our offices to discuss financial program options.
How long is the CDL course?
The CDL A course is 4 weeks and the CDL B course is 3 weeks. Click HERE for course details.
What happened to Pacific NW Professional Driving?
We changed our name in the beginning of 2022 to better reflect our uniqueness and bold commitment to our students.
Do you offer night classes?
We offer extended hours at some locations in the summer as the weather gets nicer and the sun stays out longer. This allows us to accommodate students with irregular schedules.
Do I have to attend every day for 8 hours?
Yes. The best way to learn and retain the required skills and knowledge is to commit your efforts to a full-time curriculum which includes 8-hour class days.
Do you offer weekend classes?
We do not offer weekend classes at this time.
Can you get me a job?
We do offer job placement assistance and have relationships with many hiring companies around our locations.
Can I use my own truck for the class?
We supply trucks and equipment needed for the course and final exam.
How long are your trailers?
We primarily use 28-foot trailers during our CDL course. However we do have larger equipment that is used for advanced courses.
