CDL Courses

Our programs are approved by both states. We have over 40 years experience as truckers and truck driving instructors.

what do we offer

Our CDL Courses have been developed to optimize our students' time at school and thoroughly prepare them for a successful final CDL exam. The programs adhere to all state and federal standards and include cutting edge technology and teaching techniques to help all levels of drivers develop strong, safe, skills and knowledge.

Courses offered (may vary by location):

  • CDL – Class A (160 hours)
  • CDL – Class B (80 hours)
  • Bus & Passenger Endorsements
  • Hazmat Endorsement

Class A CDL Program

Our Class A CDL Program is a 4-week (160 hour) course with the following curriculum:

week

01

Classroom theory, regulations, and introduction to trucking:

  • Introduction to trucking
  • Hours of Service
  • Control Systems
  • Vehicle Inspection
  • Vehicle Systems
  • Basic Control
  • Shifting
  • Backing
  • Coupling and Uncoupling
  • Visual Search
  • Communication
  • Speed & Space Management
  • Night Driving
  • Extreme Driving Conditions
  • Hazard Awareness
  • Emergency Maneuvers
  • Skid Control
  • Accident Procedures
  • Recognizing and Reporting Malfunctions
  • Personal Health
  • Trip Planning

week

02

Field training & Lab Introduction:

  • Pre-Trip Inspection
  • Equipment familiarization
  • Introduction to Backing (straight line, off-set maneuvers)
  • Introduction to shifting
  • Introduction to city drives

week

03

Field training & Lab Skill Building:

  • Pre-Trip Inspection
  • Backing skills building (straight line, off-set, 90 Degree)
  • Shifting skills
  • City and highway drive practice
  • Scored Lab assessments

week

04

Field training & Lab Proficiency:

  • Pre-Trip Inspection
  • Backing skills building (straight line, off-set, 90 Degree)
  • Shifting Proficiency
  • City and highway drive proficiency
  • Scored Lab assessments
  • Final Exam preparation
