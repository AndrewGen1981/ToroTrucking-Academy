Why Us ?

TORO Trucking Academy, registered as a CDL Provider with Washington State, offers a nationally-recognized training program in Tacoma. You will join the thousands of successful graduates in the trucking industry.

You will learn from trainers with decades of industry experience as drivers and company owners. We know the industry inside and out, and make sure you benefit from our experience. We offer you:

  1. Our management owned a fleet of 35 trucks with seven-figures in annual sales.
  2. We have a great placement rate with carefully selected top trucking companies.
  3. We are state-approved, and provide lots of financial assistance options and flexibility.
  4. We offer job placement services to all our students!
  5. We are VA approved and can accept the GI Bill®️️ for CDL Class A & B Training.

  7. Hazmat and Bus training is in addition to the regular CDL course.
